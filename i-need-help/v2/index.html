Others, I’ve observed, wait until it’s offered. They struggle, but they don’t necessarily try to hide it. “Oh, little old me over here? Do I need help? Well shucks, if you’re offering!” This is maybe the best scenario, because you can be sure that you’re not bothering anyone. Just make sure your dramatics aren’t too obvious; otherwise, it’s basically the same as petitioning for help, but in a much less straightforward way. And that’s more annoying than just having asked in the first place.

– Mabel Chan
